-People most commonly drink coffee to relieve mental and physical fatigue and to increase mental alertness. Coffee is also used to prevent Parkinson disease, dementia, and many other conditions, but there is no good scientific evidence to support many of these uses.

-Coffee consumption is used for social activity, leisure, improvement of work performance, and well-being. Coffee is not only a medicinal alternative but also a beverage containing numerous potential health benefits.



#Cold Coffee

Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee normally and then serving it over ice or in cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold.

Main Ingredient: Coffee, Ice.

#Cappucinno

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Austria with later development taking place in Italy, and is prepared with steamed milk foam.

Main Ingredient:Steam milk foam, Hot Milk, Double Espresso.

#Americano

Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, traditionally brewed coffee.

Main Ingredient: Hot Water,Espresso.

#Latte

Caffè latte, often shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee beverage of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk.

Main Ingredient: Milk, Espresso.
